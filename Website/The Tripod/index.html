The Tripod is Misty, a black and white kitten we found hiding in the bushes in our neighbor's yard. When she first saw us, she tried to run away. That's when we saw she had a broken and infected back leg that was just dragging behind her as she moved. Fast forward to the vet that same day, who had to amputate. It will be two years in July, 2018 since we found her, or she found us! She is the princess of the household and literally zips around the house on her 3 legs.



Here she on the first day we found her, all 4 legs intact, but you can clearly see that back leg is not doing so well. She must have been in a lot of pain, not to mention starving, thirsty and terrified! She was about 2 months old.

After her surgery the day after we found her, the doctors were already in love with her due to her spunky attitude. She came home the day after that to a new home, warm bed and lots of food and love. Here is she asleep in daddy's arms, where she definitely knew she was safe.

Yep, she climbed into the Christmas tree! She knows she's the prettiest ornament and showing off her ear tufts too!
