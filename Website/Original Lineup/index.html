Original Cat Pack "The Feline Brady Bunch" 1992-2012

When my husband and I got married in 2008, we each brought 2 cats into the new family!

Riley: Our Oreo cookie!
Rusty: This is how he slept!
Sarah: The expressive talker!
Nala: Now the oldest and sweetest!

My girls Riley and Sarah came to live with my husbands' Rusty and Nala in 2008. Sadly, we lost Riley (17), Rusty (15) and Sarah (12) to various feline illnesses. They will be forever in our hearts and are missed every day!

The youngest of the "Original Lineup", Nala (now 14), is now the eldest of the whole current crew!
