The Trio are our three brown striped tabbies from the same litter. They were abandoned by their mother and found by a cat-lover who fostered them for a few weeks until we adopted them at 5 weeks old.

Merry

The Non-Alpha Male

Merry is the largest of the trio and named after a Hobbit in "Lord of The Rings"

He loves to cuddle, jump up wherever he can and tries to run outside every time the door opens.

Nicknames: Merryberry, Merry the Man, Crybaby, Moo-Moo

Pippin

The Water-Bearer

Pippin is lighter in color than his brother and sister and is the only one in The Trio not to have ear tufts.

He is mesmerized by water and always wants to be around a running sink or glass of water. He is the most athletic of our cats.

Nicknames: Pipsqueak, The Pipster, Pippydips, Boo. He is named after Merry's sidekick in LOTR.

Gali

The Cookie Monster

Gali is short for Galadriel, another LOTR character.

She is most persistent in getting her cat treats, or "cookies", instead of regular cat food. She is overweight as a result.

Nicknames: Babygirl, Goo-Goo, Gali Girl, Little Seal
